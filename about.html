About the Project

Hooked on Security is a web-based phishing training platform designed specifically for Drexel University faculty members. Our platform provides a safe, interactive environment where faculty can practice identifying phishing attempts without real-world risks.

Why It Matters?

  • Proactive Defense: Shift from reactive measures to proactive education.
  • Realistic Scenarios: Simulations based on actual phishing attacks.
  • Instant Feedback: Immediate analysis of user actions.
